## Key Decisions
- Polygon.io free tier (5 req/min, no daily cap) over Alpha Vantage (25 req/day cap)
- SWR 30s polling over Socket.io (Vercel-compatible, simpler)
- Decimal(15,4) for all prices (not Float)
- StockAnalytics separate table (not computed on-demand)
- PushSubscription @@unique([userId, endpoint]) for multi-device support
- Cron 21:30 UTC for US (handles EST/EDT post-close always)
